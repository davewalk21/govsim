- Goals
    Win an election
    Win a supermajority
    Start a civil war
    Start a war with some other country
    Absorb other countries (canada, mexico, greenland, england, etc.)
    Change the type of government
        create a dictatorship (or transition to other type like parliment, kingdom, or other)
        delete one of the 4 parts of government (executive, senate, house, judicial)
